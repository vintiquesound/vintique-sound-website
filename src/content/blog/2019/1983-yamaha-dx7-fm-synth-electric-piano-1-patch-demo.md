A demo of the "E.Piano 1" factory patch from the "Master Group" collection. I play three different performances, with and without the  sustain pedal, and with and without stereo delay.

This thing sounds gorgeous. My plan is to produce an entire album under my DJ alias "Antoku" using only the DX7, a Yamaha TX816, and an Akai S3000 sampler. Vintage vibes here we come!
